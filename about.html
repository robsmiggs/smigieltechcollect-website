The story behind Smigiel Technologies and Collectibles.

Meet Robert

Technology has always been more than a career for me—it has been a lifelong interest. I have spent years designing, implementing, supporting, and troubleshooting enterprise technology environments. Along the way, I earned nine Microsoft certifications, including an MCSE background.

Collecting grew from that same curiosity and attention to detail. I enjoy learning the history behind an item, understanding condition and authenticity, tracking the market, and helping others make informed decisions.

Smigiel Technologies and Collectibles brings those two worlds together. The common thread is trust: accurate information, honest communication, and care for the things people value.

My approach: explain what I know, be clear about what I do not know, price fairly, and treat every customer and collector with respect.
